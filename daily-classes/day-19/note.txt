Day:- 19 (Units)


Units
1. Px (pixels) - fixed
2. % - depends on parent
3. vh (viewport height) - depends on viewport’s height. 1vh = 1% of viewport width; 50vh = 50% of screen height; same goes for vw
4. vw (viewport width) - depends on viewport’s width
5. vmax (viewport maximum) - depends on maximum viewport
6. vmin (viewport minimum) - depends on minimum viewport
7. em - depents on size of element’s parent; if parents size is 20px then 1em = 20px, 2em = 40px.
8. rem - depends on size of root element (<html>)
